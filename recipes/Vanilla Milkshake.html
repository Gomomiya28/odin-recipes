Vanilla Milkshake

Description

Ready to escape into a frozen fantasy that will take you to milkshake paradise? The Ultimate Vanilla Milkshake is here! We believe in going all out, so this wonderful combination consists of silky vanilla ice cream, a dash of milk, a cloud of whipped goodness, colorful sprinkles, and a cherry on top.

Ingredients

  • 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 cups) of vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • For Garnish:Whipped topping, Sprinkles and Maraschino Cherry

Preperation

  1. Blend vanilla ice cream and milk until smooth.
  2. Pour into a milkshake glass.
  3. Top with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a maraschino cherry.
