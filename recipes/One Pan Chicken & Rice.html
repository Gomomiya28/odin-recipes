One Pan Chhicken & Rice

Description

One Pan Chicken & Rice is a straightforward one-pan meal that glows with vibrant Mediterranean flavors. Fluffy rice is baked with tender chicken thighs that have been marinated in a zesty, lemon-dijon sauce.

One pan meals are the best. They simplify supper preparation and are filling enough to serve as leftovers. Although it's a rare feat, consider yourself lucky if you have any of this excellent meal's leftovers.

Ingredients

    Mediterranean Chicken Marinade

  • Chicken
  • Lemon
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Oregano and Thyme

    • Rice

  • Long Grain White Rice
  • Chicken Stock
  • Baby Spinach
  • Onion
  • Dried Oregano

Preperation

  1. Marinate the chicken: Whisk the marinade, pour over the chicken, and marinate in the fridge.
  2. Sear the chicken: In a large skillet, cook the chicken thighs on both sides until golden. Then remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Cook the vegetables and herbs: Saute the onions, spinach, and garlic with oregano, salt, pepper, and the reserved marinade. Give everything a stir until the spinach is slightly wilted.
  4. Bake in the oven: Add the rice and broth to the skillet and bring it to a simmer. Place the chicken thighs on top, cover the skillet, and bake for about 35 minutes. Then, remove the lid and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish and serve: Let the chicken rest, then garnish with parsley and lemon zest!
